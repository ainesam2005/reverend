About the Reverend

A Shepherd Called to Serve

Reverend Patrick is the ordained minister of ST Paul Katete Parish, Church of Uganda. With a heart anchored in scripture and a calling rooted in community, he has dedicated his life to shepherding the people of Katete with wisdom, compassion, and faith.

Ordained under the Church of Uganda — a member of the worldwide Anglican Communion — Rev. Patrick leads worship, pastoral care, discipleship programmes, and community outreach that reaches across the Katete district and beyond.
